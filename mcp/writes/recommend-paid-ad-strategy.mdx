Returns ranked coverage-gap recommendations for paid placement on AI engines (ChatGPT Ads today).

## Algorithm (Coverage-Gap v1)

- **Step A** — competitor Google-Ads keyword spend (optional input)
- **Step B** — non-branded prompts where the brand's SoV < 30% (`is_branded=false` filter)
- **Step C** — double-signal: prompts that have BOTH competitor spend AND Citable visibility gap surface first
- **Step D** — LLM-ready ad-group seeds with recommended `context_hints`

## When to use

Use this when the user asks:

- "Where should I spend on ChatGPT Ads?"
- "Recommend a paid-ads strategy"
- "What prompts am I losing to competitors that they're paying for?"
