Returns ranked coverage-gap recommendations for paid placement on AI engines (ChatGPT Ads today).

## How it ranks

- **Step A** — pull competitor keyword spend from Google Ads (optional input).
- **Step B** — surface non-branded prompts where the brand's share of voice is below threshold.
- **Step C** — double signal: prompts with BOTH competitor spend AND a Citable visibility gap surface first.
- **Step D** — return ad-group seeds with recommended context hints.

## When to use

- "Where should I spend on ChatGPT Ads?"
- "Recommend a paid-ads strategy."
- "What prompts am I losing to competitors that they're already paying for?"
