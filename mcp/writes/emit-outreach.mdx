Emit an outreach action: validates it against the persona's guardrails, creates/updates the outreach reply, and records the action for later outcome measurement.

Supported channels:
- 'reddit' / 'hn' / 'twitter' / 'linkedin_listening' (social replies — fully wired)
- 'gmail' / 'linkedin_dm' (returns status='pending_connector' until those connectors are available)

Use this when:
- The user wants to reply to a Reddit / HN / Twitter / LinkedIn thread that's been queued for response.
- An ingested conversation is ripe for a follow-up reply (pair with from_conversation_id).

Skip when:
- The outreach is broad content publishing (use emit_content).
- The outreach is paid surfacing (use emit_paid).

Persona guardrails: persona_id + intent are REQUIRED. A blocking violation halts the call unless override_reason (≥ 8 chars) is provided; overrides are recorded.

Inputs:
- persona_id (required): persona the reply speaks as.
- intent (required): 'engagement' (relationship-build) or 'commercial' (pitch/conversion).
- channel (required): one of the listed channels above.
- reply_text (required, 1-4000 chars): the body of the reply.
- source_ref_id (required for social channels): the Reddit/HN/Twitter/LinkedIn thread id this responds to.
- source_url (optional): the source thread URL for audit.
- brand_account_id (optional): the brand account this reply posts as.
- scheduled_for (optional, YYYY-MM-DD): land on the calendar at a specific date.
- override_reason (optional, ≥ 8 chars): bypass block-severity violations with audit.

Response: \{ actionId, replyId, channel, status: 'queued' | 'scheduled' | 'pending_connector', validation \}.
