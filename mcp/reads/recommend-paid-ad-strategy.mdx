Returns ranked coverage-gap recommendations for paid placement on AI engines (ChatGPT Ads today).

How it ranks:
- Finds non-branded tracked prompts where the brand's share of voice is under 30% — visibility gaps worth buying.
- When you pass competitorKeywords (e.g. from a Google Ads export), prompts competitors are already paying for rank first: a visibility gap plus competitor spend is the strongest buy signal.
- Each recommendation comes back as a ready-to-use ad-group seed with suggested targeting hints.

Use this when the user asks:
- "Where should I spend on ChatGPT Ads?"
- "Recommend a paid-ads strategy"
- "What prompts am I losing to competitors that they're paying for?"

## Inputs

| Field | Type | Required | Default | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `engine` | `'chatgpt' \| 'perplexity' \| 'gemini' \| 'grok' \| 'copilot'` | no | `"chatgpt"` | AI ad engine to plan for. Default chatgpt. |
| `days` | `integer` | no | `30` | Visibility lookback window in days (7-90). Default 30. |
| `competitorKeywords` | `object[]` | no | — | Optional competitor keyword-spend rows (e.g. from a Google Ads export). Prompts with matching competitor spend rank first. |
