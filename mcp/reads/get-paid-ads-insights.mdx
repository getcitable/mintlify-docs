title: "get_paid_ads_insights"
description: "Returns the four-metric funnel plus paid metrics for the brand's paid placements on AI engines."
---

Returns the four-metric funnel (Citation Rate, Mention Rate, Average Rank, Sentiment) plus paid metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, CPC, CTR) for the brand's paid placements on AI engines.

## When to use

- "How's my ChatGPT Ads campaign doing?"
- "What's my paid AI visibility?"
- "Compare branded vs non-branded paid performance."

## Inputs

The `split` input controls whether the response is a single funnel or a branded-vs-non-branded breakdown.
