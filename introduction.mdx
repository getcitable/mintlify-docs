Citable is the AEO/GEO platform for B2B brands. When a prospect asks an AI assistant "what's the best tool for X" or "which company should I use for Y," the assistant answers from a small set of trusted sources. Citable measures whether your brand is in that set, recommends what to do about it, and helps you ship the work.

It's built for founders, marketing leaders, and content teams who already know SEO is changing — and who want to be cited by the next generation of search before competitors are.

## Why it matters

AI assistants are becoming the new front door to B2B research. The buyer who used to type a query into Google and click through ten blue links now asks ChatGPT to compare three vendors and recommend one. If you're not cited, you're not in the consideration set.

The brands that win this surface are the brands that:

- Show up across multiple AI engines, not just one
- Have content that AI assistants actually trust enough to quote
- Get mentioned alongside category leaders, not below them
- Move citation rate up over time, not just hold steady

Citable is built around those four outcomes.

## How a typical week looks

1. Your scan runs in the background and updates your dashboard.
2. The Action Engine shows you the three highest-impact things to do this week.
3. You approve them. Citable drafts the content, lines up the spokes, and queues the work.
4. You ship — post the LinkedIn, publish the blog, file the technical fix.
5. Thirty days later, you see what moved.
